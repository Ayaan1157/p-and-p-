/ The Studio

Sensory elegance meets raw physical precision.

We believe architecture is not just building a physical shell, but choreographing spatial energy. Operating at the intersections of structural boldness and intricate detailing, Paper & Pencil creates highly custom environments.

Our projects range from high-capacity industrial plants like John Distilleries in Goa to luxury high-rise apartments such as Peninsula Heights in Bangalore. Each site represents a bespoke balance of functional efficiency, raw material honesty, and quiet luxury.

/ Our Methodology

Form is schematic; detail is absolute.

01

Vision & Brief

We collaborate closely with clients to outline operational demands, sensory aspirations, and budget limits, mapping spatial trajectories before drawing lines.

02

Schematic Design

Generating site plan alignments, architectural elevations, and photorealistic 3D light studies, solidifying the layout forms and overall visual geometry.

03

Tactile Styling

Curation of actual wood panels, detailed stone slabs, custom carpentry joinery, electrical light layouts, and fabrics to design an atmosphere of quiet luxury.

04

Construction Admin

High-precision on-site engineering coordination, structural review, and finish quality control, ensuring that the completed site matches our design.
